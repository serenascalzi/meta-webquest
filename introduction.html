Introduction

Why can learning be so difficult? We have all had the experience of taking a class in which we struggle to understand the information being taught. We think that it is some failing on our part or, worse, that we just aren’t smart enough. But the truth is that we all learn in different ways.

Do you know what learning style you prefer? Do you know what strategies to use with that style? Through this WebQuest, you will answer these questions and create a learning profile that will help you to succeed in college and beyond. You will then present your learning profile using one of your preferred learning strategies.

Learning can be difficult, but it doesn’t have to be—not if you understand the ways in which you communicate and learn! Click on the Tasks section to get started.
