Presentation Requirements

  • Your Learning Preferences and Scores from the VARK Questionnaire
    • Visual
    • Aural/Auditory
    • Read/Write
    • Kinesthetic
  • Your Learning Strategies and Scores from the Strategies Questionnaire
    • Visual
    • Aural/Auditory
    • Read/Write
    • Kinesthetic
  • Three (3) or More Characteristics of Your Preferred Learning Styles
  • Three (3) or More Strategies Associated with Your Preferred Learning Styles
  • Three (3) or More Links to your Online Research

Rubric

  • Learning Preferences and Scores
    • Full Points - Learning Preferences and Scores Included
    • Partial Points - Learning Preferences or Scores Included, but Not Both
    • No Points - Neither Learning Preferences nor Scores Included
  • Learning Strategies and Scores
    • Full Points - Learning Strategies and Scores Included
    • Partial Points - Learning Strategies or Scores Included, but Not Both
    • No Points - Neither Learning Strategies nor Scores Included
  • Learning Style Characteristics
    • Full Points - Three (3) or More Learning Style Characteristics Included
    • Partial Points - Either Two (2) or One (1) Learning Style Characteristic Included
    • No Points - No Learning Style Characteristics Included
  • Learning Style Strategies
    • Full Points - Three (3) or More Learning Style Strategies Included
    • Partial Points - Either Two (2) or One (1) Learning Style Strategy Included
    • No Points - No Learning Style Strategy Included
  • Online Research
    • Full Points - Three (3) or More Online Research Links Included
    • Partial Points - Either Two (2) or One (1) Online Research Link Included
    • No Points - No Online Research Links Included
