Evaluation

Requirements

Your presentation should include each of the items below, along with your name and your contact information. Submit a link to your presentation online to complete this WebQuest.

  • Your learning preference and four scores from the VARK Questionnaire
  • Your learning strategies and four scores from the Strategies Questionnaire
  • 3 (or more) characteristics of your learning preference
  • 3 (or more) learning strategies that you can use
  • 3 (or more) sites that you visited for information
