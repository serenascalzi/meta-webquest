Activity #1

There are many assessments available to discover your learning style and strategies, but we will be using VARK®. These assessments, developed by Neil D. Fleming and Charles C. Bonwell, are free for students and help to determine how you learn and communicate best. Learn more about the questionnaires and their evolution by watching the videos below.

After watching these videos, complete The VARK Questionnaire and record your results. Be sure to include your learning preference (visual, aural, read/write, kinesthetic, etc.), as well as your scores for each preference.
