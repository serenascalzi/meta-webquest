Activity #2

There are four learning preferences—visual, aural (or auditory), read/write, and kinesthetic. Students with a visual preference learn by seeing and drawing. Students with an aural (or auditory) preference learn by hearing and speaking. Students with a read/write preference learn by reading and writing. Students with a kinesthetic preference learn by experiencing and moving. Students with a preference for more than one style are multi-modal. Learn more about these learning preferences by watching the videos below.

After watching these videos, complete the Strategies Questionnaire and record your results. Be sure to include your learning strategies (visual, aural, read/write, kinesthetic, etc.), as well as your scores for each strategy.
