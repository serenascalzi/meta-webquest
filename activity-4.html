Process

Activity #4

After watching the videos, completing the assessments and the research, and recording your results, compile all of the information that you have gathered into a learning profile. Once you have assembled your profile, present it using one of your preferred learning strategies. If you are a visual learner, you might create a graphic in Canva or a chart in Lucidchart. If you are an aural (or auditory) learner, you might record a presentation in Google Slides or a discussion in Zoom. If you are a read/write learner, you might assemble lists in Google Docs or documents in LiveBinders. If you are a kinesthetic learner, you might make a game in Scratch or a video in YouTube. If you are a multi-modal learner, you might use any of these ideas. The resources below are free for students and can help to get you started.

  • Visual Learners
    Canva | Lucidchart
  • Auditory Learners
    Google Slides | Zoom
  • Read/Write Learners
    Google Docs | LiveBinders
  • Kinesthetic Learners
    Scratch | YouTube
