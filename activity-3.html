Activity #3

There are strategies to use with each of the four learning preferences—visual, aural (or auditory), read/write, and kinesthetic. Visual strategies include color-coding notes and creating diagrams. Aural (or auditory) strategies include reading aloud and participating in discussions. Read/write strategies include taking notes and creating lists. Kinesthetic strategies include being active and using examples. Multi-modal strategies incorporate some or all of these, depending on your preferences. Learn more about these learning strategies by watching the video below.

After watching this video, conduct an online search for your learning preference and strategies. Be sure to record at least 3 characteristics of your learning preference, at least 3 learning strategies that you can use, and at least 3 sites that you visited for information.
