Task

Here are the tasks that you will complete during this WebQuest.

  • Task #1
    Learn about the different learning styles and determine the style that you prefer.
  • Task #2
    Learn about the different learning strategies and determine the strategies that you are using.
  • Task #3
    Research your preferred learning style and the learning strategies to use with it.
  • Task #4
    Present the results of your research using one of your preferred learning strategies.
