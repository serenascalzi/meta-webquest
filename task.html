Task

First, you will learn about the different learning styles and determine the style that you prefer.

Second, you will learn about the different learning strategies and determine the strategies that you are using.

Third, you will research your preferred learning styles and the learning strategies associated with it.

Fourth, you will present the results of your research using one of your preferred methods of learning.
